> [!abstract] Theorem.
> 
> Let $\lbrace A_i \rbrace$ be a collection of [events](statistics/event.md). Then
> $$\left(\bigcup_i A_i\right)^c = \bigcap_i A_i^c \quad\text{and}\quad\left(\bigcap_i A_i\right)^c = \bigcup_i A_i^c.$$
