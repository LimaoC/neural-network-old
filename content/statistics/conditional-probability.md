## Definition
The [event](statistics/event.md) that $A$ occurs, given that we know $B$ has occurred, is denoted $A | B$. We have
$$\mathbb{P}(A | B) = \frac{\mathbb{P}(A \cap B)}{\mathbb{P}(B)},$$
where $\mathbb{P}(B > 0)$.
